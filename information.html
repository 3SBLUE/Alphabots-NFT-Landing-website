MORE THAN JUST AN NFT
The Alpha Bots aren’t just here to look cool, they want to earn their stay in the physical realm. By implementing a fair voting system, The Alpha Bots return power to the holders by letting them have a say in the direction of the community, and ultimately the project.
The community wallet is a great example of The Alpha Bots making an impact. Whether the community decides to make even more charitable donations or even if they decide to sweep the floor, the choice will be with the community.
A BIT MORE ABOUT THE TEAM
The team behind the Alpha Bots project is comprised of a diverse range of talent. The design and marketing team members all work from various locations in the UK, whilst our development and PR functions both sit in Canada.
We’ve worked hard to bring Alpha Bots to the Solana blockchain this quickly, and we are super excited for the partnerships that will be launching soon. We are all hard workers, but we know how to have fun too. When Alpha Bots take over – expect more than a community boat party!
We intend to give back to our community through reinvestment of the proceeds from royalties, including repurchasing some of our collections and giving those NFTs out as community rewards. We will be taking a percentage of our secondary sales every month into repurchasing other NFT projects that our community will vote upon to build our treasury.
While we are building our own community within the NFT space, we want the NFT and crypto community to know that we are doing this to make the industry a better place and give back to the community that made all this happen.
